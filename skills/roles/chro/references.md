# CHRO — references & further reading

Operator-grade HR / people / employment-law references.

---

## Classification

- **US — IRS guidance on independent contractor classification.** IRS Publication 15-A; the three-factor common-law test.
- **California — AB5 / ABC test.** Cal. Lab. Code § 2750.3. The strict California classification framework.
- **UK — IR35.** HMRC guidance on intermediaries legislation.
- **EU — varies by member state.** Each country's labor authority publishes its own framework.

## Hiring and interviewing

- **Brendan Reid's "Hiring Engineers" and similar operator playbooks.**
- **First Round Review** (firstround.com/review) — operator-grade essays on hiring at startups.
- **High Growth Engineering** (Jordan Cutler) — interview design, scoring, calibration.
- **Lou Adler — "The Essential Guide for Hiring & Getting Hired"** — performance-based interviewing.
- **Sourcing patterns:** LinkedIn Recruiter, GitHub, Read.cv, Wellfound (formerly AngelList Talent), founder networks.

## Performance management

- **Kim Scott — Radical Candor** (radicalcandor.com). Operator-grade performance conversations.
- **Andy Grove — High Output Management.** Classic management text; performance frame still applicable.
- **HBR archive on PIPs and difficult conversations** (hbr.org).
- **SHRM (Society for Human Resource Management)** for HR-professional standards (shrm.org).

## Compensation

- **Levels.fyi** for tech salary comparisons across companies, levels, geographies.
- **Pave** (paywall) for real-time anonymized startup comp benchmarks.
- **Comprehensive** (paywall) for public startup salary data.
- **Carta Total Comp** for equity + comp benchmarks if using Carta.
- **OptionImpact** / **J. Thelander Compensation Reports** for early-stage equity data.
- **BLS** (bls.gov) for government-published US labor statistics.

## Employment law

- **US: Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA), 29 U.S.C. § 201 et seq.** Wage and hour.
- **US: Title VII, ADA, ADEA, FMLA, ERISA, GINA, USERRA.** Federal employment protections.
- **State employment law** — varies enormously. California Department of Industrial Relations (dir.ca.gov), NY DOL, etc.
- **EU: Working Time Directive, etc.** Per member state.
- **UK: Employment Rights Act 1996.** Plus subsequent acts.

(For specific legal questions, route through CLC; this is operator-grade framing only.)

## Org design

- **Team Topologies** (Skelton & Pais, 2019). Patterns for team structure and interaction.
- **Andy Grove — High Output Management.** Foundational org design thinking.
- **Lattice + Culture Amp** for performance + engagement tooling.

- **Gusto** (gusto.com) — best UX for tiny teams.
- **Rippling** (rippling.com) — best at 30-150 employees.
- **Justworks** (justworks.com) — PEO for 10-100 employees.

- **Sequoia One** — PEO with benefits brokerage.
- **Deel** (deel.com) — international EOR (Employer of Record).
- **Remote.com** — international EOR.
- **Multiplier** — international EOR.
- **ADP** — for 150+ employees, traditional payroll.
- **Workday HCM** — enterprise HRIS.
- **BambooHR** — HRIS for 50-500 employees.

## Equity tooling

Employment law changes frequently and jurisdictionally. Cite this skill as operator-grade framing, not current statute. For any active legal question, route to CLC + jurisdiction-specific employment counsel.

Comp data ages quickly. Refresh from current sources at least annually for any band you maintain.
